SPATIAL SPOTTING/TECHNICAL DEMO

BY NOTERIK / VERSION 0.1 / FEB 2014

The Spatial Spotting technical demo is part of Noterik's ongoing development work on the Multiscreen Toolkit. The demo has bin made in order to test the ability to look at video's together (play,pauze and scrub together) and users being able to pin-point certain object on the screen to each other.
